The Future of Personal Knowledge Management

For decades, we have been collecting bookmarks, screenshots, and notes scattered across dozens of apps. The promise of a "second brain" remains largely unfulfilled — not because the tools are inadequate, but because the friction of capture remains too high.

What if saving a page were as effortless as starring a tweet? What if AI could pre-digest content the moment you save it, so your future self always finds organized, summarized notes — never raw clutter?

This is the bet behind a new generation of clipping tools...
